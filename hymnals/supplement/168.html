Revelation 4 V11 Thou Art Worthy

Thou art worthy, thou art worthy, thou art worthy O Lord, Thou art worthy to receive glory, Glory and honor and power, For thou hast created, hast all things created, For thou hast created all things, And for thy pleasure they are created, Thou art worthy O Lord.
