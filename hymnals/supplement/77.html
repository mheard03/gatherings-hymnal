Farther Along

Tempted and tried, we're oft made to wonder, Why it should be thus all the day long; While there are others living about us, Never molested, though in the wrong.

Farther along we’ll know all about it, Farther along we’ll understand why; Cheer up, my brother, live in the sunshine, We’ll understand it all by and by.

Faithful till death,” saith our loving Master; A few more years to labor and wait; Toils of the road will then seem as nothing, As we sweep through the beautiful gate.

Farther along we’ll know all about it, Farther along we’ll understand why; Cheer up, my brother, live in the sunshine, We’ll understand it all by and by.

When we see Jesus coming in glory, Gather us to him up in the sky; Then we shall meet him and praise him ever, We’ll understand it all by and by.

Farther along we’ll know all about it, Farther along we’ll understand why; Cheer up, my brother, live in the sunshine, We’ll understand it all by and by.
