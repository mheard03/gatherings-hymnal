Thessalonians 5 V16-25 Rejoice Evermore

16. Rejoice ever more for this is the will of God, Rejoice ever more for this is the will of God, Rejoice ever more for this is the will of God, in Christ Jesus concerning you.

17. Pray without ceasing for this is the will of God, Pray without ceasing for this is the will of God, Pray without ceasing for this is the will of God, in Christ Jesus concerning you.

18. In everything give thanks for this is the will of God, In everything give thanks for this is the will of God, In everything give thanks for this is the will of God, in Christ Jesus concerning you.

19. Quench not the Spirit for this is the will of God, Quench not the Spirit for this is the will of God, Quench not the Spirit for this is the will of God, in Christ Jesus concerning you.

20. Despise not prophesyings for this is the will of God, Despise not prophesyings for this is the will of God, Despise not prophesyings for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus concerning you.

21a. Prove all things for this is the will of God, Prove all things for this is the will of God, Prove all things for this is the will of God, in Christ Jesus concerning you.

21b. Hold fast that which is good for this is the will of God, Hold fast that which is good for this is the will of God, Hold fast that which is good for this is the will of God, in Christ Jesus concerning you.

22. Abstain from all evil for this is the will of God, Abstain from all evil for this is the will of God, Abstain from all evil for this is the will of God, in Christ Jesus concerning you.

25. Brethren pray for us for this is the will of God, Brethren pray for us for this is the will of God, Brethren pray for us for this is the will of God, in Christ Jesus concerning you.
