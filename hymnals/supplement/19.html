Praise the Lamb

Blessed Lord, our hallelujahs now to thee we raise; Never could we fully utter all thy worth and praise!

Praise the Lamb, for he is worthy! Sweet eternal strain. Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Praise the Lord! Amen.

Praise the Lamb! Yes, thou art worthy, who didst shed thy blood, To redeem thy saints, and make us kings and priests to God!

Praise the Lamb, for he is worthy! Sweet eternal strain. Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Praise the Lord! Amen.

Hallelujah, thou, Lord Jesus, canst not cease to love; Thine we are, and thine forever, one with thee above.

Praise the Lamb, for he is worthy! Sweet eternal strain. Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Praise the Lord! Amen.

Praise the Lord! Yes, hallelujah! Who would hush the song? Join with saints from every nation, every tribe and tongue.

Praise the Lamb, for he is worthy! Sweet eternal strain. Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Praise the Lord! Amen.
