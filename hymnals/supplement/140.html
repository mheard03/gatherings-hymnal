Psalm 16 -- The Lord is the Portion

The Lord is the portion of mine inheritance, And of my cup,

Thou wilt show me the path of life: In thy presence is fullness of joy; At thy right hand there are pleasures for evermore. For I have set Jesus always before me: He's at my right hand.

Therefore is my heart glad, my glory rejoiceth, My flesh rests in hope.
