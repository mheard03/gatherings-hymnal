Unto Him

With praise and thanksgiving, there stands a great throng In the presence of Jesus, and sing this new song:

Unto him who hath loved us and washed us from sin, Unto him be the glory forever, Amen.

All these once were sinners, defiled in his sight, Now arrayed in pure garments in praise they unite:

Unto him who hath loved us and washed us from sin, Unto him be the glory forever, Amen.

He maketh the rebel a priest and a king, He hath bought us and taught us this new song to sing:

Unto him who hath loved us and washed us from sin, Unto him be the glory forever, Amen.

How helpless and hopeless we sinners had been, If he never had loved us till cleansed from our sin:

Unto him who hath loved us and washed us from sin, Unto him be the glory forever, Amen.

Aloud in his praises our voices shall ring, So that others believing, this new song shall sing:

Unto him who hath loved us and washed us from sin, Unto him be the glory forever, Amen.
