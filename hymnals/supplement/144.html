Psalm 27-V7-9 Hear O Lord When I Cry

Hear, O Lord, when I cry with my voice, Hear, O Lord, and have mercy, Have mercy also upon me, Hear, O Lord, and answer me. When thou saidst, seek ye my face, My heart said unto thee, Thy face, O Lord, will I seek, Hide not thy face far from me.

Psalm 30 Thou Hast Turned My Mourning

Thou hast turned my mourning into dancing for me;Thou hast put off my sackcloth; Thou hast turned my mourning into dancing for me, And girded me with gladness; To the end my glory may sing praise unto thee, And not be silent. O Lord my God, I will give thanks unto thee forever.
