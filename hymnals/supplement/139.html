Psalm 3 My Glory and the Lifter of My Head

My glory and the lifter of my head, My glory and the lifter of my head. But thou, O Lord, art a shield for me; My glory and the lifter of my head, I cried unto the Lord with my voice, I cried unto the Lord with my voice, I cried unto the Lord with my voice, And he heard me out of his holy hill.
