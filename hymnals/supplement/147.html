Psalm 51 -- Create In Me A Clean Heart

Create in me a clean heart, O God, And renew a right spirit within me. Create in me a clean heart, O God, And renew a right spirit within me.

Cast me not away from thy presence, And take not thy holy spirit from me. Restore unto me, the joy of thy salvation, And uphold me with thy free spirit.

Then will I teach transgressors thy ways, And sinners shall be converted unto thee, Deliver me from blood-guiltiness, O God, Thou God of my salvation.

And my tongue shall sing a loud, Of thy righteousness, O Lord, Open thou my lips, and my mouth shall shew forth thy praise, Thou God of my salvation.
