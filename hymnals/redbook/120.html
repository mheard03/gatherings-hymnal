Opened for Me

There is a Fountain opened for my cleansing, Where sin’s atonement by my Lord was made; He was the Lamb that was led to the slaughter, His blood the fountain where my debt was paid.

Opened for me, Opened for me, The precious, cleansing Fount was opened there for me

There is a Rock that stands, by storms unshaken, Redemption’s Author the foundation laid; By faith my stand on His righteousness I’ve taken, He will not fail I shall not be dismayed.
