O Where are Kings and Empires Now

O where are kings and empires now Of old, that went and came? But Lord, Thy Church is praying yet, A thousand years the same.

We mark her goodly battlements And her foundations strong; We hear, within, the solemn voice Of her unending song.

For not like kingdoms of the world Thy holy Church, O God, Tho earthquake shocks are threat’ning her, And tempests are abroad.

Unshaken as eternal hills, Immovable she stands, A mountain that shall fill the earth, A house not made with hands.
