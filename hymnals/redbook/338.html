O Grant Thy Touch

O grant Thy touch of fire, Lord, And set my heart a-flame, Until I’ve one desire, Lord - To glorify Thy name.

O grant Thy touch of peace, Lord, And heal my troubled mind; May faith and trust increase, Lord, With fear and doubt behind.

O grant Thy touch of joy, Lord, Until its spring shall flow Unchecked – tho things annoy, Lord, And sorrow’s sting I know.

O grant Thy touch of strength, Lord, My given place to fill – To go to any length, Lord, To do Thy blessed will.
