The Great Creator

How I praise the great Creator, Who did make this world so fair; As I scan the realm of nature I can see His image there.

In the steep and on the hilltop, There I find His royal throne; And the God who made the mountain Ever loves me as His own.

Tiny bud and lovely flower, All the fragrant bloom of spring, Show the sweetness and the beauty Of our Sovereign and my King.

Bubbling spring and swirling river, Lapping lake and tossing sea, All declare the might and greatness Of the One who care for me.

How I praise the great Creator, Who did make the world so fair, For I too am His creation, And I love Him for His care.
