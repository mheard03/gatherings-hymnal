Sooner or Later

Sooner or later the skies will be bright, Tears will be all wiped away; Sooner or later, then cometh the light, Night will be turned into day.

Sooner or later cares will have flown, Sunshine and gladness we’ll see Sooner or later God calleth His own, With Him forever to be.

Sooner or later, our Lord knows the hour, He’ll send His beloved Son; Sooner or later, in His might and power, Our battles all will be won.

Sooner or later cares will have flown, Sunshine and gladness we’ll see Sooner or later God calleth His own, With Him forever to be.

Sooner or later, yes, sooner for some, Darkness will all then be past; Sooner or later our Savior will come – With Him will be your lot be cast?

Sooner or later cares will have flown, Sunshine and gladness we’ll see Sooner or later God calleth His own, With Him forever to be.
