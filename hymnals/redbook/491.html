Heavenly Joy is Ringing

Heavenly joy is ringing, And our hearts are singing, For His blood hath bought us – When condemned, He sought us.

Men of every nation Sing the great salvation of our blessed Jesus, Who in mercy freed us. O the joy before us, There is heavenly chorus; Praises we’ll be blending For all time unending!

Hail, Thou blessed Savior! We thru Thee find favor Thou for us art pleading, Ever interceding.

Now with sins Forgiven We can enter heaven; There we’ll all adore Thee, Sovereign King of glory.
