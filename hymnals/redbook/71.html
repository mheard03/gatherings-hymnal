Blessed Day of Rest and Cheer!

Blessed day of rest and cheer! Day divine, to me so dear! When we gather old and young, Joining here in prayer and song.

Now the week of toil is o’er, And in peace we sit once more In The presence, gracious Lord, Waiting for Thy hold Word.

Lord, our God, we seek Thy face, Bless us with sustaining grace; May Thy heralds everywhere Faithfully Thy truth declare.

Let Thy mighty Word hold sway Over allour hearts today; Hungering souls, good Shepherd, feed, Into pastures green us lead.

May, O Lord, the day be near, When we pass from trials here Into Thine eternal rest, In the mansions of the blest.
