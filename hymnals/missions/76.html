Lord Lay Some Field Upon My Heart

Lift up your eyes, look on the fields, they white to harvest are, And God is calling volunteers to serve him near and far.

Lord, lay some field upon my heart, I And love that field through me. And may I nobly do my part, to win that field for thee.

And you dear Christian, God now calls, to labor for the lost. Will you your life, your gifts, your all, give him at any cost?

I will be true, My Lord to serve, who died to set me free. I'll consecrate my life to him, and ever faithful be.
