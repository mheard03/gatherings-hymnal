We‘ll Work Till Jesus Comes

Oh land of rest for thee I sigh, when will the moment come, When I shall lay my armor by, and dwell in peace at home? We’ll work till Jesus comes, we'll work till Jesus comes, We’ll work till Jesus comes, and we'll be gathered home.

The hosts of hell the world have filled, and kept the nations bound, We‘ll seek thy face and do thy will, to pull the strongholds down. We‘ll pray till Jesus comes, we'll pray till Jesus comes, Well pray till Jesus comes, and then be gathered home.

Lord, send me to the lands of night, where sin and darkness reign, And through me flood them with thy light, till thou dost come again. This world is bound for hell, can we refuse to tell? We'll point them to the cross, until he calls us home.
