Have Thine own Way

Have thine own way, Lord, have thine own way. Thou art the potter, I am the clay. Mold me and make me after thy will, While I am waiting, yielded and still.

Have thine own way, Lord, have thine own way. Wounded and weary, help me, I pray. Power, all power, surely is thine, Touch me and use me, Saviour divine.

Have thine own way, Lord, have thine own way. Hold o'er my being absolute sway. Fill with thy Spirit till all shall see, Christ only, always, living in me.
