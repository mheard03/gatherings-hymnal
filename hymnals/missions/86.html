Hear O Hear

Hear, O hear the Saviour calling, who will work for me today? Souls are dying in the dark-ness, who will point them to the way?

Hear O hear the voice of Jesus, He is calling you and me. Let us answer, quickly answer, Saviour, we will go for thee.

Go and tell them 'ere they perish, tell them of a Savior’s love. They have never heard the message, tell them of a home above.

Hear O hear the voice of Jesus, He is calling you and me. Let us answer, quickly answer, Saviour, we will go for thee.

Some from every tribe and nation must be gathered to the fold. Go and tell them, hasten, hasten, let the story now be told.
